Struggle finding clothes for your morning?

Here is your solution...

f-Hanger（倚鱼）comes for your busy morning. It leans on the wall elegantly and saves much space for the bedroom. By preparing your clothes the night before, you won't struggle to find the clothes to wear in the morning.
